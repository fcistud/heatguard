# Saudi Arabia midday work ban

## Rule (as applied in HeatGuard)

- **Country code:** SA (Saudi Arabia)
- **Season:** 15 June – 15 September
- **Daily prohibited window:** 12:00 – 15:00 (local time)
- **WBGT condition:** None

## Policy gap

Same structural limitations as other GCC calendar bans: fixed season dates, fixed noon window, no acclimatization differentiation.

HeatGuard's **Riyadh demo** (Open-Meteo archive, focus day 15 July 2024) illustrates humid **morning** danger hours the ban misses while **needlessly stopping** some safe noon hours.
